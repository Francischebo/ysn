Signature Annual Events

These flagship programs occur each year across Kenya, anchored in YSN's mission

Youth & Constitution Week

August - Our flagship legal literacy program aimed at empowering young Kenyans with constitutional knowledge.

  • Legal literacy sessions in schools and universities
  • Community debates, quizzes, and essay competitions
  • Social media challenge: "Know Your Rights Kenya"

Healing Circles Camp

October - A wellness-focused retreat for students dealing with various forms of stress and pressure.

  • 3-day wellness camp for students and young leaders
  • Safe space for processing trauma, stress, academic pressure

Pads for Power Campaign

March - Our largest nationwide dignity campaign focused on menstrual health and education.

  • Largest nationwide YSN pad distribution and dignity campaign
  • Held during International Women's Month
  • Targets over 50 schools annually

University Chapter Summit

July - A leadership development program bringing together all YSN chapter leaders.

  • Gathering of chapter presidents, secretaries, and coordinators
  • Training on program design, budgeting, reporting, and leadership

Green Justice Month

September - Our environmental justice initiative focused on climate literacy and action.

  • Tree planting drives, climate literacy workshops, clean-up events
  • Youth leaders trained on environmental governance
